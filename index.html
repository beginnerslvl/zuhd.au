Uniting Global Scholars for a Spiritual Journey Across Australia!

Join the esteemed Sheikh Hassan Ali on his captivating tour across Australia, visiting Melbourne, Sydney, and Perth. A distinguished British Muslim scholar, Sheikh Hassan Ali masterfully combines traditional Islamic teachings with Western academic approaches, offering invaluable perspectives on current issues faced by Muslims in the West. Seize this exclusive chance to learn from a respected scholar, deepen your appreciation of Islam, and engage with fellow Muslims in a dynamic, knowledge-seeking community.

Enrich your child's spiritual journey with our engaging afterschool Islamic classes at Maktab, held at Featherbrook College, Monday to Thursday from 6:30 pm to 8:00 pm. These interactive sessions offer a nurturing environment for kids to explore the teachings of Islam, deepen their understanding of the faith, and develop a strong foundation in Islamic values. Our dedicated instructors guide and inspire young learners, fostering a sense of community and connection to their rich Islamic heritage.

Discover the inspiring life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) at The Seerah Series, held every Friday at 2:15 pm at the Wyndham Library, Point Cook. Delve into the captivating journey of the beloved Prophet, exploring his teachings, character, and the foundations of Islam. These weekly sessions offer an enriching experience for attendees, providing valuable insights and fostering a deeper connection to the faith. Join us as we embark on this spiritual exploration, celebrating the remarkable legacy of the Messenger of Allah (PBUH).

About us
