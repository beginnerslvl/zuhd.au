Project

The construction of a new mosque, Islamic school, Darul Ifta, and other facilities in Point Cook, Melbourne, Australia, is an exciting development for the Muslim community in Australia. This project will provide a much-needed space for Muslims to come together, worship, and learn about Islam in a supportive and welcoming environment. It will be the first project of its kind to make an entire Islamic Village with all facilities included in it taking inspiration from Darul Uloom Kaurangi of Mufti Taqi Usmani in Karachi, Pakistan.

Financial donations can be made online through the zuhd.au website or by contacting the project's fundraising team directly (info@zuhd.au). Donors can choose to make a one-time donation or set up recurring payments.

Donations are a critical component of this project's success, as the construction and operation of these facilities require significant funding. Individuals and organizations can contribute to this project by making financial donations, providing in-kind donations, or volunteering their time and expertise.

In-kind donations, such as building materials, equipment, or professional services, are also welcome and can be arranged by contacting the fundraising team (info@zuhd.au). Volunteers can contribute their time and skills to help with fundraising events, community outreach, or other aspects of the project.

The benefits of donating to this project are manifold. By contributing to the construction of the mosque and Islamic school, donors will be supporting the education and spiritual development of Muslims in the Point Cook as well as Muslims in Australia as a whole. The project will also provide a space for the broader community to learn about Islam and build relationships with their Muslim neighbours, promoting interfaith understanding and harmony.

Furthermore, by supporting this project, donors will be investing in the future of the Muslim community in Australia. The facilities will enable the community to grow and thrive, providing a solid foundation for generations to come.

In summary, the Point Cook Mosque project presented by zuhd.au is an important development for the Muslim community in the area, and donations play a crucial role in its success. Individuals and organizations can contribute to this project through financial donations, in-kind donations, or volunteering, and by doing so, they will be supporting the education and spiritual development of Muslims in the community and investing in the future of the Muslim community in Australia.
